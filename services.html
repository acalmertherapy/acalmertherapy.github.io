Oncology Massage - For men and women

1 hour 10 minute Appointment · $90

Helpful for all stages of cancer treatment and recovery, oncology massage is a safe and effective technique that can relieve pain and stress for many people. In 2006, while supporting my husband through treatment for cancer, I witnessed the benefits of massage as a complementary therapy for cancer patients, often referring to massage as "A Calmer Therapy" because it had such a profound impact without interferring with medications. Now trained as an oncology massage therapist, I am pleased to offer this speciality to men and women in all stages of cancer treatment and recovery.
